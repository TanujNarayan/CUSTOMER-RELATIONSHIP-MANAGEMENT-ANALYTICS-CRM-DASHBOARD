**CRM Analytics – Opportunity & Leads Dashboard**
This project highlights my ability to transform CRM data into actionable business intelligence. By analyzing opportunities and leads, I built dashboards that reveal conversion trends, pipeline health, and sales performance insights. The work demonstrates end‑to‑end analytics skills across data preparation, SQL exploration, visualization, and executive storytelling.

**Tools Applied**
-Excel
-Power BI
-Tableau
-MySQL

**Core Activities**
Standardized and enriched CRM datasets (leads, opportunities, pipeline stages).
Conducted exploratory analysis using SQL and Excel.
Designed interactive dashboards in Power BI, Tableau, and Excel.
Measured KPIs including lead quality, conversion ratios, opportunity progression, and revenue impact.
Delivered a final presentation with strategic recommendations for sales optimization.

**Deliverables**
.pbix — Power BI dashboard
.twbx — Tableau dashboard
.xlsx/.csv — Processed CRM dataset
.pptx — Executive presentation deck
SQL scripts (optional inclusion)

**Insights & Outcomes**
Pinpointed high‑value opportunities and lead sources driving revenue growth.
Diagnosed pipeline inefficiencies and stage‑wise drop‑offs.
Revealed sales trends and contribution by lead category.
Proposed data‑driven strategies to enhance CRM effectiveness and boost conversions.
